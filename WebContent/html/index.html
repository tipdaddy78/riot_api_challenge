A World of AP seeks to demonstrate the change in AP Item usage due to the large item rework. This is done by showing the pick rates and win rates of the changed items across 10,000 games per region for patches 5.11 and 5.14. Additionally, you can see how the changes to an item affected a certain Lane, and Champion. This data can be viewed on a per region basis. Additionally, global comparisons may be made on the "World View" page.

To get started: Click a region on the map below (or in the menu if you are on a smaller screen or tablet/mobile device) to see what effect the item changes had on their usage of those items.
If you are on mobile, switch to landscape orientation for the best viewing experience.
